Restriction site associated DNA markers (RAD tags) are used in association with high-throughput sequencing methods for genotyping in both model and non-model organisms .

RAD tags are produced by using restriction enzymes to cut an organism DNA and to perform a genome complexity reduction : only fragments associated with restriction sites are sequenced. The distribution of sequenced fragments along the genome can be adjusted by carefully choosing the restriction enzyme.
